Rodney Mullen

The Einstein of Skateboarding

Here are some of his achievements throughout his life:

  • 1966 - August 17th Rodney Mullen was born in Gainsville, Florida.
  • 1977 - January 1st Rodney began skateboarding under the pact that he always wear pads and to quit if he were to get injured.
  • 1977 - August 24th he entered his first competion and placed third, earning him a sponsorship from Walker Skateboards.
  • 1980 - August 15th he places first at the Oasis Pro Skateboard Competion in San Diego. He joins the Bones Brigade.
  • 1982 - January 1st Powell-Peralta releases Join the Brigade: Skateboarding in the '80s featuring Rodney Mullen.
  • 1983 - Rodney lands what he calls the "magic flip" which later evolves into the kickflip today
  • 1987 - August 1st World Industries is established with the help of Steve Rocco.
  • 2000 - May 17th Rodney Mullen releases a patent for a proprietary skateboard truck which eliminates adverse characteristics like wheel bite.
  • 2002 - May 28 he is presented with the award of Reader's Choice at the Fourth Annual TransWorld Skateboarding Awards.
  • 2003 - January 1st Almost Skateboards is established.
  • 2005 - He patents a skateboard that is comprised of carbon fiber to improve stiffness and durability
  • 2005 - August 2nd, Rodney unveils his first book, "The Mutt: How To Skateboard and Not Kill Yourself".
  • 2013 - May 10th Rodney Mullen is inducted into the Skateboard Hall of Fame

I played a little baseball, did a few other things like that, but I always had the sense of being an outsider, and it wasn't until I saw pictures in the magazines that a couple other guys skate, I thought, 'Wow, that's for me,' you know?
